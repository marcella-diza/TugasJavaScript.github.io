About Us

Welcome to Calla Choffee, where every cup tells a story. We believe coffee is more than just a drink—it's a way to bring people together. From the aroma of freshly roasted beans to the friendly smiles of our baristas, we aim to create a cozy corner for your daily escape. Whether you're here for a quick caffeine boost or a slow morning chat, you'll always find warmth in every cup.

Our Story

It all began with a simple dream—to create a place where coffee lovers and friends can gather. From humble beginnings, **COFFEE TIME** has grown into a community hub, serving handcrafted drinks and freshly baked treats. Our commitment to quality and hospitality remains the heart of everything we do.
